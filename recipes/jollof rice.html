Jollof recipes

Description

Recipe on how to prepare Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • Two cups of long grain rice
  • Fivelitre of water
  • One tin tomato
  • Three cubes of seasoning
  • One sachet oil
  • One spoonful of pepper
  • One bulb of onions
  • Two kilos of chicken
  • One spoonful of salt
  1. Boil the rice for 15 minutes
  2. Then bring rice down from fire and leave to drain
  3. Fry your chicken to desired texture
  4. Fry your tomato for 30 seconds
  5. Add the rest of the Ingredients
  6. Leave to fry for 15 seconds
  7. Then add your rice and water
  8. Cook till Satisfied
  9. Serve with your fried chicken and your jollof rice is ready
